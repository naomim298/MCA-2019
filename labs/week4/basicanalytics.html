Week 4: Basic Analytics

Each of the three graphs represent pitch in different formats and are based on features I extracted which I thought were most relevant to my piece, using jSymbolic. Here, is the file containing the features that I extracted.

Piano Roll of Pitches

This graph shows where and which pitches occur alone or simultaneously throughout the 10 measures of the song provided. Note B seems to occur the most consistently, appearing in measures 3, 4, 6, 7, 8. Whereas, note F only occurs once briefly in measure 8.


Pitch Histogram

The second graph shows at which count that certain octaves occur more frequently. For exmaple, C2 occurs at the highest counts of about 48 and 55.


Scatter Plot of Pitches

The Scatter plot shows the pitches that occur at different quarter lengths. There seems to be a much greater freqeuncy occuring at Quarter Length 0.5 with all pitches occring at this time aside from D. Wheres at Quarter Length 3.5, the frequency is much less with only 3 pitches occuring: B, E and A.
